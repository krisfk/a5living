Simple & Easy UI. This plugin will alert you via email notifications when your site is down and back live. It checks the uptime and downtime of your website. 

== Description ==

You will get email notification alert as soon as your website is down and back live using this FREE, Simple & very easy to setup plugin. We use our Uptime monitoring service to check your domain regularly and alert you as soon as we find your website is down.
This plugin will also help you monitor the website's uptime in a given period of time using the premium plan. It would make website maintenance easier and you wouldn't have to manually check your website's status.

<h4>FREE Plugin Feature</h4>
* **Site-Uptime Monitoring:** It helps you know when your website or server is down so that you can take necessary steps before your customers face issues. You can easily monitor your website's uptime and downtime using this plugin.
* **Email Notification:** We send an email alert when we detect that your website is down so that you can take necessary steps. We will also alert you when your site is back up. 
* **Instant Detection:** Our service checks your domain regularly so that we can alert you as soon as your website is down.

== Installation ==


= From your WordPress dashboard =

1. Navigate to `Plugins > Add New` from your WP Admin dashboard.

2. Search for `Site Uptime Monitoring`.

3. Install `Site UpTime Monitoring` and Activate the plugin.



= From WordPress.org =

1. Search for `Site UpTime Monitoring` and download it.

2. Unzip and upload the `Site UpTime Monitoring` directory to your `/wp-content/plugins/` directory.

3. Activate Site UpTime Monitoring from the Plugins tab of your admin dashboard.


== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Once Activated =

1. Select miniOrange SiteUptime from the left menu and follow the instructions.

2. You can enter your email. You will be alerted whenever your website goes down and is back to live.
